# Nykaa Social Media & Customer Review Analysis

## Project Overview
Nykaa is a leading Indian fashion, beauty, and lifestyle e-commerce platform operating in a highly competitive digital market. Customer perception, social media engagement, and online reviews play a critical role in influencing purchase decisions and brand loyalty.

This project analyses "social media conversations and customer reviews" related to Nykaa to evaluate "brand perception, customer satisfaction, market trends, and competitive positioning". The goal is to convert unstructured customer feedback into "actionable business insights and strategic recommendations".

---

## Business Problem
Despite strong brand recognition, Nykaa faces increasing competition from major e-commerce platforms and D2C brands. Understanding customer sentiment, service gaps, and emerging trends is essential for improving customer experience, strengthening market position, and sustaining growth.

---

## Business Objectives
- Assess Nykaa’s "brand perception" across social media platforms  
- Analyse "customer sentiment" using online reviews and discussions  
- Identify "emerging trends" in beauty and fashion segments  
- Evaluate Nykaa’s "competitive position" against key competitors  
- Provide "data-driven strategic recommendations" for business improvement

## Methodology

### Data Collection
- Collected customer opinions from multiple social media platforms  
- Extracted customer reviews from review websites  
- Identified recurring themes related to products, delivery, pricing, and customer service  
- Categorized feedback as "Positive, Negative, or Neutral"

---

### Brand Perception Analysis
- Analyzed online discussions to understand overall brand image  
- Identified key brand attributes such as authenticity, trust, quality, and pricing perception  

---

### Customer Review & Sentiment Analysis
- Evaluated drivers of customer satisfaction and dissatisfaction  
- Identified major pain points including:
  - Delivery delays (especially in Tier 2/3 cities)  
  - Refund and return process inefficiencies  
  - Slow or ineffective customer support  

---

### Market Trends Identification
- Monitored social media activity to track emerging beauty and fashion trends  
- Observed growing interest in:
  - Sustainable and cruelty-free products  
  - Ethnic and seasonal fashion collections  
  - Influencer-driven product discovery  

---

### Competitive Landscape Assessment
- Benchmarked Nykaa against major competitors:
  - Amazon Fashion  
  - Flipkart  
  - AJIO  
  - Myntra  
- Compared platforms based on:
  - Product assortment  
  - Pricing strategies  
  - Customer satisfaction  
  - Marketing and influencer presence  

---

## Key Insights
- Nykaa enjoys "strong brand trust and product authenticity", particularly in beauty categories  
- Negative sentiment is largely driven by "post-purchase issues", including refunds and customer support  
- Competitors outperform Nykaa in "pricing and delivery speed" 
- Influencer marketing significantly influences customer purchase decisions  
- Rising demand for "eco-friendly, wellness, and cruelty-free products"

---

## Strategic Recommendations
- Improve "customer service response time" and issue resolution mechanisms  
- Simplify and standardize the "return and refund process"  
- Enhance "delivery transparency and tracking systems"  
- Strengthen influencer collaborations aligned with trending categories  
- Expand sustainable, wellness-focused, and private-label product offerings
